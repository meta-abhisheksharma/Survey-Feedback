About Us

Its Oky to make a mess, experiments can lead to beautiful things.

Everybody wants to grow and for a growth they need opinion, views, suggestion, feedback. Our team worked in the direction to meet all these requirements. Survey Feedback is meant to ask (many) people a question or a series of questions in order to gather information about what most people do or think about something. It is a series of questions aimed at extracting specific data from a particular group of people. Surveys may be conducted by phone, mail, via the Internet, and sometimes face-to-face on busy street corners or in malls. Survey research is often used to assess thoughts, opinions, and feelings. Conducting surveys seems simple enough: ask people questions, record their answers, and report the results. Even Roman Emperors polled their citizens in ancient times. Any survey helps companies, organizations and individuals alike to gather the insights they need to make more smart,informed decisions. The Internet has become a powerful method of gathering information from consumers; voice recognition software allows businesses to offer rewards to consumers who call in to respond to surveys throughout the day.

Survey Feedback is ideal for anyone who needs to collect valuable information from customers, visitors or employees efficiently. It is a project intended for creating a survey of any kind, answering the questions asked in survey and viewing survey reports.
